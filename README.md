## 🤖 What I'm Building

### SomMemo · Trustless On-Chain Digital Will 🏆
> *Top 1 of Top 10 — Somnia Reactivity Hackathon*

**Business case:** Crypto is lost forever when an owner passes away — there's no trustless way to pass it on without handing over your keys. SomMemo lets you name a beneficiary and deposit assets; if you stop "checking in" before a deadline, the assets transfer automatically. A digital will, fully on-chain.
**Technical edge:** Keeper-free automation using Somnia's Reactivity scheduling precompile (no Chainlink/Gelato dependency), reentrancy-hardened with the CEI pattern, written in Solidity and tested with Hardhat.
**Links:** [Repo](https://github.com/0xpochita/SomMemo)

### Tirai · Privacy-First Bug-Bounty Payouts 🏆
> *5th Place — Frontiers Hackathon 2026 (Track Cloak)*

**Business case:** Whitehat hackers usually have to reveal their wallet to claim a bounty, exposing their identity and earnings. Tirai severs the on-chain link between a project's deposit and a researcher's withdrawal, so they get paid anonymously.
**Technical edge:** I built the backend & SDK layer — wrapping Cloak's zero-knowledge Shield Pool into a safe API and a wallet-only auth-server (EIP-191 signatures + JWT) on Solana.
**Links:** [Repo](https://github.com/alventendrawan123/tirai)

### MonzuPay · On-Chain Streaming Payroll
> *Built at PL_Genesis: Frontiers of Collaboration Hackathon (Flow track)*

**Business case:** Payroll is batched monthly — employees wait weeks for money they've already earned, and cross-border transfers are slow and expensive. MonzuPay streams salary per second, lets employees claim anytime, and puts idle funds to work earning yield.
**Technical edge:** I built the Cadence smart contracts on Flow — per-second salary streaming, autonomous scheduled payroll via FlowTransactionScheduler (no external keepers), and DeFi yield routing through IncrementFi.
**Links:** [Repo](https://github.com/0xpochita/monzupay) · [Live Demo](https://monzupay.vercel.app)

### Redelegator Finance · Autonomous DeFi Delegation
> *Built at the MetaMask Smart Accounts Kit × 1Shot API × Venice AI Dev Cook-Off*

**Business case:** Autonomous DeFi bots usually demand blanket token approvals — one compromise drains the whole wallet. Redelegator grants a single scoped permission that's redelegated down a chain of AI agents, each with cryptographically enforced, ever-narrowing spending caps.
**Technical edge:** I built the delegation backend & SDK on MetaMask Smart Accounts (ERC-7715/7710, EIP-7702) — narrowing redelegation, gasless redemption via a 1Shot relayer, and a one-call kill-switch, all zero-custody. Tested end-to-end on Base mainnet.
**Links:** [Repo](https://github.com/0xpochita/redelegator-finance)
